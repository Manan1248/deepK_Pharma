# EmailJS Setup - Get Your Public Key

Follow these 3 simple steps to activate email sending:

## Step 1: Go to EmailJS
Visit: https://www.emailjs.com/

## Step 2: Sign Up (FREE)
- Click "Sign Up" button
- Use your Gmail (mananbhalani00000@gmail.com)
- Complete the signup

## Step 3: Get Your Public Key
1. After login, go to **Dashboard**
2. Look for "Public Key" section on the left
3. Copy your **Public Key** (it looks like: `abc123xyz456`)
4. Save it somewhere

## Step 4: Update Your Website
1. Open **script.js** file
2. Find this line (around line 8):
   ```
   emailjs.init('YOUR_EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY');
   ```
3. Replace `YOUR_EMAILJS_PUBLIC_KEY` with your actual public key
4. Save the file

## Step 5: Connect Gmail Account
1. In EmailJS Dashboard, go to **Email Services**
2. Click **Add Service**
3. Choose **Gmail**
4. Connect your Gmail account (mananbhalani00000@gmail.com)
5. Done!

## That's It!
Your forms will now send emails to your inbox automatically when customers submit.
